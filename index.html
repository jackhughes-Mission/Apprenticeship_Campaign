What is a Change, Projects and
Business Analysis Apprenticeship?

Change, Projects and Business Analysis specialists explore new technologies to solve business problems, and implement them in a strategic way to transform the businesses goals, processes and technologies.

This apprenticeship is aimed at creative, innovative and strategic individuals who have a passion for technology. You don’t need to have any previous work experience in the sector, you will gain this from your apprenticeship - all we ask is that you are ambitious, passionate and willing to learn.

You will be working across a variety of Government departments on projects which help transform the way the processes and technologies run within government. You’ll have the opportunity to take part in career workshops, soft skills training and team building experiences. This is a great apprenticeship to kickstart your career, gain valuable work experience and study for your L4 Business Analyst apprenticeship qualification.

You won’t be on your own and will be supported by a team of dedicated mentors who were once in your shoes and are now trusted employees and who can help you get the most out of this experience.

FAQs

What is the application process?

The application process will run across three stages leading up to a September 2024 start.

  • The first stage is to complete the online application which includes information about yourself and a couple of short tasks.
  • Your application will then be reviewed and should you be successful, you will be invited to participate in the second stage and attend a group Assessment Workshop inCentral London. There will be 2x options of dates which you can select between and you will be asked which might work best for you within your online application.
  • If you are successful after the Assessment Workshop you will move to the final screening stage for suitability when you'll be invited to meet the team and find out more information, leading towards a September 2024 start.
